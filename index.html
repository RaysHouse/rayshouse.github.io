Welcome to the Ray's House Foster Home Official Site!

We are a group of volunteers looking to start a foster home in the Inland Northwest, nearby Spokane, WA. We hold annual yard sales and all proceeds go towards purchasing a home to convert into a foster home. Our goal is to be a functioning non-profit independent foster home in the Eastern Washington region.
