High Level Mission Statement

Our mission at Ray's House is to offer a safe, stable, and temporary home for children in crisis, no matter the reason.


At Ray's House we are dedicating ourselves to:

    • Providing a safe environment.
      Quality nutritional care.
      A natural, homelike environment.
      Mental, emotional & physical needs met to the best of ability daily.
      Positive experience with adults and peers.
